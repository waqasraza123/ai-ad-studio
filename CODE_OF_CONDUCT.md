
Code of Conduct
Our standard

AI Ad Studio aims to be a respectful, constructive, and professional project space.

By participating in this repository, you agree to engage in ways that support a welcoming and productive environment for everyone.

Expected behavior

Examples of behavior that help this project:

communicating with respect and clarity

assuming good intent while reviewing work critically

giving actionable feedback instead of dismissive feedback

accepting responsibility for mistakes

focusing discussion on the work, not the person

helping maintain a safe and collaborative environment

Unacceptable behavior

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

harassment, intimidation, or threats

hateful, discriminatory, or demeaning language

personal attacks or repeated hostile arguing

trolling, bad-faith disruption, or deliberately derailing discussion

publishing private or sensitive information without permission

spam, impersonation, or abusive contact

sexualized language or unwanted advances

Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

issues

pull requests

discussions

review comments

commit messages

any project-managed communication space related to this repository

Maintainer responsibilities

Maintainers may edit, remove, lock, or otherwise moderate content that violates this code of conduct.

Maintainers may also warn, temporarily restrict, or permanently remove participants whose behavior repeatedly harms the project environment.

Reporting

To report behavior that violates this code of conduct, use the contact route documented in SUPPORT.md or the private reporting path in SECURITY.md when the situation includes sensitive or abusive behavior.

Please include:

what happened

where it happened

who was involved

any supporting screenshots or links

why you believe it violates the code of conduct

Reports will be reviewed in good faith and handled as privately as practical.

Enforcement

Possible enforcement actions include:

request for correction

warning

content removal

conversation lock

temporary restriction

permanent ban from participation

Final note

The goal of enforcement is to protect the project and keep collaboration productive, safe, and professional.
